# OSTrack Privacy Policy (Draft)

Last updated: 2026-04-09
Status: Draft for legal review

Related plan: [LEGAL_REVIEW_PLAN.md](LEGAL_REVIEW_PLAN.md)

## 1. Summary
OSTrack is a social cataloging platform for soundtrack discovery and discussion. This draft explains what data we process, why we process it, and your controls.

## 2. Data We Collect
- Account data: username, email, profile image.
- Auth data: identity provider metadata from Auth0.
- Activity data: follows, shelf updates, ratings, timeline contributions.
- Device/app data: basic diagnostics and error events.
- Consent records: timestamped consent changes for policy compliance.

## 3. Data We Do Not Intend To Collect
- Raw payment card data.
- Raw IP addresses in consent logs (hash only where required).
- Sensitive category data unless explicitly required by law and separately consented.

## 4. Why We Process Data
- To provide account and login functionality.
- To deliver core app features (shelves, ratings, social feed).
- To maintain service reliability and prevent abuse.
- To comply with legal obligations.

## 5. Legal Basis (GDPR)
- Contract: providing the OSTrack service.
- Legitimate interest: security, abuse prevention, diagnostics.
- Consent: optional recommendation profiling and marketing communications.
- Legal obligation: required compliance records.

## 6. Consent and Controls
- Essential processing is required for account operation.
- Recommendation profiling is optional and can be disabled.
- Marketing consent is optional and can be withdrawn at any time.

## 7. Retention
- Account and activity data is retained while the account is active.
- Deleted accounts are soft-deleted first, then anonymized/deleted per retention policy.
- Security and audit logs are retained for a limited period based on operational need.

## 8. Sharing
We share data only with service providers required to run OSTrack (for example hosting, auth, monitoring, analytics) under contractual safeguards.

## 9. International Transfers
If data is processed outside your region, OSTrack will use appropriate transfer safeguards.

## 10. Your Rights
Depending on jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, export, and object to certain processing.
