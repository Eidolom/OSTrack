# Legal Review Plan

Last updated: 2026-04-09
Status: Draft

## Purpose
Convert the current Privacy Policy and Terms of Service drafts into publication-ready documents after legal review.

## Review Inputs
- [PRIVACY_POLICY_DRAFT.md](PRIVACY_POLICY_DRAFT.md)
- [TERMS_OF_SERVICE_DRAFT.md](TERMS_OF_SERVICE_DRAFT.md)
- Phase 0 ERD sign-off notes
- Data retention and consent assumptions from the roadmap

## Review Questions
- Are the data categories and purposes described accurately?
- Are the consent and retention statements consistent with the product behavior?
- Are any jurisdictions or age restrictions missing?
- Are the contact addresses final and monitored?
- Is the governing law section acceptable for publication?

## Review Steps
1. Send both drafts to counsel.
2. Collect annotated feedback.
3. Apply requested edits.
4. Verify cross-document consistency.
5. Produce publication-ready markdown files.
6. Archive the reviewed drafts with version tags.

## Final Outputs
- Publication-ready Privacy Policy markdown
- Publication-ready Terms of Service markdown
- Changelog or version note for legal updates

## Acceptance Criteria
- Counsel approves the final wording.
- No unresolved placeholders remain.
- Privacy and Terms reference the same product behavior and contact details.
